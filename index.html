Fraud & Fake ID Warning

Submitting fake IDs, using someone else's Aadhaar without permission, or claiming winnings with stolen UPI IDs can be treated as fraud and forgery under Indian law.

  • IPC 420 — cheating / dishonest inducement.
  • IPC 468 — forgery for the purpose of cheating.
  • IPC 471 — using a forged document as genuine.
  • IT Act 66D — cheating by personation using computer resources (e.g. online identity fraud).

TrueWin reserves the right to blacklist accounts, share logs with lawful authorities, and permanently deny participation to anyone suspected of these actions.
